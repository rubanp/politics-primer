About Politics Primer

Politics Primer was created as a resource for people in the United Kingdom, primarily those have not studied Government and Politics at school, to gain an understanding of and interest in politics. Concepts are explained using a bottom up approach, focusing on the underlying principles and philosophy. The aim was to create a resource that can be understood by a wide age range, and can provide a basis for further exploration and research into politics. I believe it is as free from bias and neutral as is possible in a political guide.
