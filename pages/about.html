About Politics Primer

Politics Primer was created as a resource for secondary school students in the United Kingdom to gain not only a basic understanding in politics, but an interest and fascination into this complex but important topic.

It does this by:

  • Making politics relevant and personal
  • Avoiding uneccessary specifics and political jargon
  • Encouraging debate and discussion
  • Creative interactive learning elements

The code and content are open source on Github and anyone is free to copy and customise it for their organisation or country.
