About Politics Primer

Politics Primer was created as a resource for people in the United Kingdom, primarily those have not studied Government and Politics at school, to gain an understanding of and interest in politics. The hope is that this will lead to a better understanding of their political views and more engagement in the political process. Concepts are explained using a bottom-up approach, focusing on the underlying principles and philosophy, something I felt was rare in most political information. The aim was to create a resource that can be understood by a wide age range and can provide a basis for further exploration and research into politics. I believe it is as free from bias and neutral as is possible in a political guide.
